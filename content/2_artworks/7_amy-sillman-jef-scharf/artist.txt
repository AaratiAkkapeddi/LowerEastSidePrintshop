Bio: [{"content":{"text":"<p>Multidisciplinary artist Jef Scharf is a designer, screen printer, installation artist and musician, who has created collaborative works with Amy Sillman, Reed Anderson, Michael Smith, and Maya Hayuk. From 1998-2011, Scharf owned and operated Kayrock Screen Printing in Brooklyn, NY, where he produced artist editions, show posters, books and commercial work. Upon his return to his hometown of Cleveland in the fall of 2011, Scharf opened Gallery Wolfy Part II, in the neighborhood of Tremont. Scharf received his BFA from the Cleveland Institute of Art in 1993, and was in residence at Skowhegan School of Painting and Sculpture in 2000.
